Psychological Intake Data (Sensitive)

This is the core of how Sincerity works and requires the most transparency:

  • Your intake text — either a freewrite or responses to our guided questionnaire. This may include information about your communication style, social energy, emotional needs, sensory sensitivities, relationship preferences, lived experiences, and dealbreakers.
  • Your compatibility dimensions — ten numerical scores (1–10 each) extracted from your intake by an AI model. These represent traits like communication preference, contact frequency, social energy, relationship pace, routine need, sensory sensitivity, reassurance needed/given, and emotional support needed/capacity.
Your full intake text and psychological profile are never shown to other users. Other users see only a compatibility percentage — a single number computed from the comparison of your stored dimensions against theirs.

AI Processing of Your Intake

When you submit your intake, it is sent to Anthropic's Claude API (a third-party AI service) for processing. Claude reads your intake and extracts the ten numerical dimensions described above. This is a single API call.

Before your intake is transmitted, you will be presented with an explicit in-app confirmation step at the moment of submission, confirming your consent to have your intake securely analyzed by our processing partner, Anthropic.

Anthropic's API processes your intake in transit but does not use customer API data to train its models, subject to Anthropic's own privacy policy at anthropic.com/privacy. After processing, your raw intake text is stored encrypted in Sincerity's database. Your compatibility dimensions are stored separately and used for matching calculations.

Messaging Data

Sincerity provides native in-app messaging between matched users. Message content and timestamps are stored on Sincerity's infrastructure. We do not read your private messages except in response to a formal abuse report.

Usage and Technical Data

We may collect standard technical information including device type and operating system (for app compatibility), crash logs and error reports (to fix bugs), and approximate usage patterns (to improve the app). We do not track swipes, browsing history within the app beyond what is necessary to operate the matching system, or precise location.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To operate the service — creating and managing your account, running the matching algorithm, displaying your profile to potential matches, and delivering in-app messages.
  • To compute your matches — your compatibility dimensions are compared against other users' dimensions using deterministic arithmetic scoring. No AI is involved in the matching step itself.
  • To send notifications — email notifications when you receive a new message (first message per conversation only, by default).
  • To ensure safety — our AI intake processing includes safety detection that may flag responses indicating risk of harm to self or others. Flagged accounts may be paused and reviewed.
  • To improve Sincerity — anonymised, aggregated usage data helps us understand how the app is used and where to improve it. We do not sell this data.

We do not use your data for advertising. Sincerity does not serve ads and does not share data with advertising networks.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area or United Kingdom, our legal bases for processing your data are:

  • Contract — processing necessary to provide the service you signed up for (account management, matching, messaging).
  • Consent — for sensitive psychological intake data, which you provide voluntarily and explicitly agree to submit for matching purposes.
  • Legitimate interests — for safety monitoring and service improvement, where these interests do not override your rights.

5. How We Store and Protect Your Data

Sincerity is built on Base44, a cloud application platform. Your data is stored on Base44's infrastructure, which applies encryption at rest and in transit (HTTPS/TLS). Your intake text is stored with additional application-level encryption.

We apply access controls so that your psychological intake and compatibility dimensions are not accessible to other users or exposed in API responses to unauthenticated parties. We do not store payment information (we do not currently charge for the service).

No system is completely secure. In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify you via email within 72 hours of becoming aware, as required by applicable law.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion — request deletion of your data (right to be forgotten)
  • Portability — request your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Restriction — request that we limit how we use your data
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw consent — where processing is based on consent (such as intake data), you may withdraw at any time by deleting your account

California residents have additional rights under the CCPA, including the right to know what categories of personal information are sold or disclosed (we do not sell personal information) and the right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@sincerity.app. We will respond within 30 days. You can also delete your account directly from within the app under Settings.
