3. How Sincerity Works

Sincerity matches users based on psychological compatibility, not appearance. Here is how the process works:

  • You complete a private psychological intake — either a freewrite or a guided questionnaire.
  • Your intake is processed by Claude (an AI model by Anthropic) in a single API call. This extracts ten numerical compatibility dimensions from your responses. Anthropic does not store your intake content beyond processing.
  • Your compatibility dimensions are stored securely and used to compute match scores against other users using deterministic arithmetic scoring — not AI similarity search or neural embeddings.
  • You see a compatibility percentage with other users. Your full psychological profile is never shown to anyone, including your matches.
  • You can initiate conversations with matches directly within the Sincerity app.
